# Code of Conduct / 社区行为准则

## English

ProofGate is a research and engineering community for verifiable redaction in hybrid LLM deployment. The community should be rigorous, reproducible, and welcoming to contributors from software engineering, security, privacy research, data governance, and applied AI.

Expected behavior:

- Discuss ideas, designs, and evidence rather than attacking people.
- Use synthetic or public non-sensitive examples only.
- State assumptions, threat models, and limitations clearly.
- Be precise when reporting privacy, utility, or security claims.
- Help new contributors find small, reviewable tasks.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Submitting real personal, customer, patient, employee, account, medical, financial, security, or proprietary data.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, model traces with real data, mapping logs, or audit logs containing real values.
- Harassment, insults, threats, discrimination, or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Presenting unverifiable claims as proven project guarantees.

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues, discussions, pull requests, comments, or commits that violate these rules. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

## 中文

ProofGate 是面向混合大模型部署中可验证脱敏问题的研究与工程社区。社区应保持严谨、可复现，并欢迎软件工程、安全、隐私研究、数据治理和应用 AI 方向的贡献者。

期望行为：

- 讨论观点、设计和证据，不攻击个人。
- 只使用合成数据或公开的非敏感样例。
- 明确说明假设、威胁模型和限制。
- 对隐私、效用和安全主张保持精确。
- 帮助新贡献者找到小而清晰的任务。

不可接受行为：

- 提交真实个人、客户、患者、员工、账户、医疗、金融、安全或专有数据。
- 公开密钥、凭据、含真实数据的模型 trace、映射日志或审计日志。
- 骚扰、侮辱、威胁、歧视或持续干扰社区协作。
- 把不可验证的说法表述为项目已经证明的保证。

维护者可以编辑、关闭或移除违反本准则的 issue、discussion、pull request、评论或提交。严重或重复违反者可能被临时或永久限制参与。
